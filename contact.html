Wait. What is WordPress?

A ThemeBeans Club membership grants you 365-day access to our complete WordPress theme collection, any and all new themes & plugins, all updates, and friendly one-on-one email support.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day, no-questions-asked refund for all WordPress themes purchased on ThemeBeans.com. If you are not satisfied with any of our products, let us know so we can make it right.

What happens if I cancel?

The themes are yours, even if your ThemeBeans Club membership expires or is cancelled. You will however lose access to downloads, future updates, new themes, or support.

Your themes will still work, though we cannot guarantee 100% capability for the latest version of WordPress if you’re using outdated themes.

Why do you have annual licenses?

It means that we are committed to our customers and active theme development.

Selling products that power your site is not a position we take lightly. It’s an ongoing commitment to continuously enhance, improve and refactor each product to ensure that your website will function at it’s peak.

How much does it cost to renew?

Annual memberships will renew at the current price. If you joined during our introductory pricing, then your renewals are also completed at that price.

Lifetime ThemeBeans Club memberships do not renew.
